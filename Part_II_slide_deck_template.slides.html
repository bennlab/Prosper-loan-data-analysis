Investigation Overview¶

Here my main focus is features that affect Effective yield(which is a form of Interest rate for the loans). I start by exploring the distribution of my main features of interest(Prosper_Score, Loan_original_amount, Monthlyloan payment and Term). Prosper Score VS Estimated Effective Yield¶

A higher Prosper score depicts a better borrower and a lower prosper score depicts a not-so-good borrower. From the plot we notice how the white dot, which is the median of estimated effective yield, drops as the Prosper score increases. This means lenders allow better interest rates to borrowers with higher rating. I best believe this acts as an incentive to keep them.

The regplot shows that as the Loan amount increases, the Effective yield decreases, however it is important to note the difference is slight and not as drastic.
